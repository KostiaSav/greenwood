Welcome to Greenwood!

Imagine yourself in a quiet forest. You left your phone in the car, and you are sitting on a log, hearing nothing but birds singing and cracks from a small fire you just made. Far from civilization, nothing bothers you. No incoming calls, no instant messages, just inner peace and calmness.

We long for these feelings, and the forest can provide them. Designed to ensure peace, Greenwood is made to take away from you all the noise, and leave you with notification sounds of rental income, while dealing with everything that’s trying / will try to disturb you.

With us you stay in control — just without the noise.
