How Greenwood Works

01

Consultation & Strategy

We start by understanding your property, your goals, and how involved you want to be. From there, we define a clear approach and identify any risks or considerations upfront.

02

Onboarding & Setup

We put everything in place for smooth, structured management. This includes agreements, documentation, a property check, and setting up communication and reporting.

03

Tenant Setup & Stabilization

Whether the property is vacant or already rented, we make sure everything is properly set. We handle leasing, tenant screening, contracts — or review the existing setup to reduce risk.

04

Ongoing Management & Reporting

We handle daily operations, maintenance, and tenant communication, keeping landlords fully visibility informed at all times via dedicated owner’s portal.
